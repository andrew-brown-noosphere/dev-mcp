# DevExp.ai - Enterprise MCP Infrastructure

AI has changed how developers discover and adopt technology. Enterprises need a platform to enable agentic developer journeys.

## What We Do

DevExp.ai builds MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers and infrastructure that make your platform AI-discoverable. When developers ask AI assistants for recommendations, your product can be discovered, tested, and integrated automatically.

## How It Works

1. **Developer asks AI**: "I need a fast database for time-series data"
2. **AI discovers your product** via MCP server
3. **AI tests your product** against their specific workload
4. **Your product wins on merit**, not marketing
5. **Developer ships** with production-ready config
