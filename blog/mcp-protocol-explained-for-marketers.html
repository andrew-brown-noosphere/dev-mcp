What is MCP?

MCP stands for Model Context Protocol. But forget the acronym. Here's what it means for your business:

MCP makes your product speak AI. Just like your website speaks HTTP to browsers, MCP lets your product speak directly to AI assistants like Claude, ChatGPT, and Cursor.
